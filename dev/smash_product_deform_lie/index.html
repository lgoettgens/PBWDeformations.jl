Smash products deformations

PBWDeformations.DeformationMap — Type
DeformationMap{C} = Matrix{FreeAssAlgElem{C}} where {C <: RingElement}

The type for deformation maps of a Lie algebra smash product. The entry kappa[i,j] should be the image of $v_i \wedge v_j$ under the deformation map, i.e. $κ(v_i,v_j)$. Deformation maps are always assumed to be quadratic and skew-symmetric.

SmashProductDeformLie struct

PBWDeformations.SmashProductDeformLie — Type

The struct representing a deformation of a Lie algebra smash product. It consists of the underlying FreeAssAlgebra with relations and some metadata. It gets created by calling smash_product_deform_lie.
